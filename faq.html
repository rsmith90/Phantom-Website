Wheel Sizes:
Wheels can range from 80mm-125mm
Competitively we see 100mm-110mm-125mm used the most

Why:
Reducing the amount of surface area on the ground at one time reduces friction, allowing skaters to feel less and roll more and glide there pushes longer.
Previously over the past 20 years wheels have increased in size from 76mm all the way to some 125mm and to even 130mm. 80mm setups used to run 5-wheels while
100mm&110mm run 4-wheel setups and 125mm-up run 3-wheel setups. Generally the further the distance the bigger the wheel, as its been demonstrated to help endurance.
